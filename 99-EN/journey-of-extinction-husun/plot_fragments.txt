Killing God: Hu Sun opens on Mount Huaguo, where the protagonist awakens among broken stone tablets and scattered relics. The old stories say that the Monkey King's power was divided after a failed rebellion against heaven.

The protagonist's core skills are the Ruyi Jingu Bang for close combat, the Seventy-Two Transformations for changing form, Somersault Cloud for rapid movement, Fiery Golden Eyes for revealing illusions, and spirit skills obtained from defeated enemies.

In the Black Wind Mountain chapter, the player learns to combine staff stances with transformation skills. Agile forms help avoid heavy attacks, while stronger forms are useful for breaking armor and interrupting bosses.

In the Snow Mountain chapter, the protagonist faces the White Bone Spirit. The battle tests illusion detection, stamina management, and the ability to read enemy patterns in a hostile environment.

In the Flaming Mountains chapter, fire-resistant equipment, precise dodging, and crowd-control skills become important. The chapter ends with a confrontation over one of the lost relics.
