Killing God: Hu Sun is an action RPG inspired by Journey to the West. The story takes place in a fictional mythic world before the heavenly order is fully settled. Demon kings rule separate territories, gods compete for influence, and the protagonist searches for the truth behind a buried origin.

The game is divided into six chapters: Black Cloud, Yellow Wind, White Snow, Purple Web, Flaming Mountains, and Unfinished. Player choices and hidden discoveries affect the ending. The protagonist's main abilities include the Ruyi Jingu Bang, the Seventy-Two Transformations, Somersault Cloud movement, Fiery Golden Eyes, spirit skills, and several transformation forms.

The world combines Chinese religious imagery, ancient architecture, mountain temples, grottoes, caves, and battlefield ruins. Combat emphasizes staff techniques, transformations, timing, evasive movement, and boss-specific strategy.
