The story of Black Myth: Wukong is divided into six chapters: Black Cloud, Yellow Wind, White Snow, Purple Web, Flaming Mountains, and Unfinished. The game has two endings, and the player's choices and discoveries influence the final outcome.

At the end of each chapter, the game presents animated sequences in both 2D and 3D styles. These scenes expand the narrative and explore the main themes behind Black Myth: Wukong.

The setting blends Chinese cultural heritage with real natural and architectural landmarks. Places such as the Dazu Rock Carvings in Chongqing, Xiaoxitian, Nanchan Temple, Tiefo Temple, Guangsheng Temple, and the Stork Tower in Shanxi appear as inspiration for the game world. The game also incorporates ideas from Buddhist and Taoist philosophy.
