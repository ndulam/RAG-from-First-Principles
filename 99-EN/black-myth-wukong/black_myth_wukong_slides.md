# Black Myth: Wukong

## Slide 1: Face Destiny

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG inspired by Journey to the West.

## Slide 2: Prologue

The player becomes the Destined One, a monkey warrior searching for the truth behind an old legend.

## Slide 3: Source of the Myth

The game adapts characters, places, and spiritual ideas from the 16th-century Chinese novel Journey to the West.

## Slide 4: Journey Begins

Players revisit mythic landscapes, fight demons, collect relics, and reshape the ending of the legend.

## Slide 5: The Destined One

The protagonist uses staff techniques, spells, transformations, and courage to face a world filled with gods and monsters.
